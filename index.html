📖 Scone Adventures

18 August 2025
Wellington Weekend: Floriditas Takes the Crown!
What an incredible weekend in Wellington! I finally made it to the legendary Floriditas Cafe, and wow - their cheese scone absolutely lived up to the hype. The perfect golden crust gave way to the fluffiest interior I've ever experienced, with chunks of aged cheddar distributed perfectly throughout. The slight tang from what I suspect was a touch of sour cream in the dough elevated this scone to championship level. At $4.50, it's premium pricing, but absolutely worth every cent. This is now my #1 ranked scone in all of New Zealand!
15 August 2025
Auckland Adventure: Astro Cafe Delivers!
Astro Cafe in Auckland has been on my list for months, and I finally got the chance to try their famous cheese scone. The presentation was stunning - golden brown with visible cheese melted on top. The texture was exceptional, with a perfect balance of fluffy interior and crispy exterior. The cheese blend appeared to include both sharp cheddar and maybe a touch of gruyere? The only minor critique is that it could have used just a pinch more salt to really make those flavors pop. Still, a solid 9.5/10 and definitely worthy of the #2 spot!
